Pokemon Cards

What is Pokemon trading card?

The Pokémon Trading Card Game was released in 1996 in Japan, and came to the US in 1999, courtesy of Wizards of the Coast. In 2003, the Pokémon Company took over publishing the game, and continues to introduce new cards for fans to catch and grow their collections.

The CGC is one of the company that help to grade card. The CGC Trading Cards Population Report includes all of the Pokémon cards graded by CGC Trading Cards, from Base Set to Sword & Shield, promo cards, starter decks and everything in between.

Collectable Gundam

What is Gundam Figure?

Gundam figures are model kits depicting the vehicles and characters of the fictional Gundam multiverse by Bandai. These kits became popular among mecha anime fans and model enthusiasts in Japan and nearby Asian countries beginning in the 1980s. Gundam modeling spread in the 1990s with North America and Europe being exposed to Gundam through television, video and manga.

Tamiya Car

What is Tamiya Car?

Tamiyais a Japanese manufacturer of plastic model kits, radio-controlled cars, battery and solar powered educational models, sailboat models, acrylic and enamel model paints, and various modeling tools and supplies. The company was founded by Yoshio Tamiya in Shizuoka, Japan, in 1946. The company has gained a reputation among hobbyists of producing models of outstanding quality and accurate scale detail.

The company's philosophy is reflected directly in its motto: "First in quality around the world". Tamiya's metal molds are produced from plans with the concept of being "easy to understand and build, even for beginners".
